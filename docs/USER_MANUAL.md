# User Manual

## Signing in

Navigate to the application URL and enter your `@sanlayan.com` email along
with the password issued by your administrator. Personal or third-party
email addresses are rejected.

## Dashboard

The dashboard shows totals appropriate to your role: equipment counts,
today's bookings, your upcoming reservations, and (for managers/admins)
recent activity plus weekly booking trends.

## Booking equipment

1. Open **Equipment**, filter by category or search.
2. Click any item to open its detail page.
3. Pick a date (today or future), start/end time between 08:00 and 20:00,
   quantity and a purpose.
4. The **Available for selected slot** counter updates live.
5. Click **Create booking** — the request is validated atomically, so
   overlapping reservations that would exceed capacity are rejected.

## Cancelling a booking

Go to **Bookings**. You can cancel your own upcoming bookings any time
before the start time. Managers and admins can cancel or return any
booking.

## Editing your profile

Open **My Profile** to update your name, phone, department, designation,
date of birth, or password.
